Post-hackathon promise · what one operator owes themselves

If I win, this becomes a real product. If I lose, the same artifact is still maintainable for years. The features below are the only ones I will keep alive past D+30 alone — everything else either earns its keep on metrics or gets sunset by D+90.

  • 4 agents · 1 Cloud Run service · 1 deploy/day max
  • 1 Cloud SQL f1-micro · 1 nightly backup · 1 alert
  • 1 RapidAPI account · 3 plans · 1 invoice
  • 1 PWA · 1 i18n bundle (KO + EN) · 1 Lighthouse target (≥ 90)
  • 1 page of metrics, on a screen I look at every Sunday
  • 1 on-call: me · pager rule: nothing wakes me unless $500 ceiling is breached
